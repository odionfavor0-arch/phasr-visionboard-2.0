Proven Strategies: Beginner-to-Beast Progression
Gym & Body Basics (Nerd Fitness Style): No gym? Bodyweight wins—10 squats, 5 push-ups (knee/wall mods), 15-sec planks, 2x daily. Warm-up 5-min (arm circles, leg swings). Progress: Add reps/sets weekly; strength training 3x/week builds muscle (boosts metabolism 5-10%). Rest smart—active recovery (walks) prevents plateaus. Common pitfalls: Too much too soon (injury), skipping form (strain).
Energy Optimization (Energie Fitness): Sleep synergy (7-9hrs) + hydration (3L water). Low-intensity steady-state (LISS) like 20-min walks elevates fat burn without fatigue. Weights 2x/week injury-proofs; slow runs keep HR low, building endurance safely.
Food for Fuel (Bodybuilding.com): 40/30/30 macros (carbs/protein/fats). Energy meals: Oats+berries breakfast (slow-release carbs), quinoa chicken salad lunch, Greek yogurt nuts snack. Nigerian tweaks: Moi moi (protein), plantain (carbs), egusi (fats). Boosters: B-vits (eggs, greens), iron (beans), avoid sugar crashes. Daily plan: Steel oats AM, tofu veg PM—sustains without dips.
Running Streaks (Run Eat Repeat): 1.6km min daily builds 5-10% oxygen uptake, fat efficiency. Alternate intensities; weights/stretch prevent overuse.
