Proven Journaling Practices: Reflect, Regulate, Release
Label & Validate: “I’m feeling [emotion] because [trigger].” Depth: “What need hides here?” (e.g., “Anger at family streak miss = need for support”).  
EQ Scan: Rate 1-10: Aware? Regulated? Empathetic?  
Box Breath: 4-in, 4-hold, 4-out, 4-hold—resets nervous system instantly.  
Reframe Ladder: Worst-case → best-case → likely → growth lesson.  
Gratitude Anchor: 3 specifics amid chaos—shifts dopamine.
